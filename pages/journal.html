01
Coffee Origins

Born in the
Highland Mist

Altitude is everything. At 1,900 metres above sea level, where clouds pool in the valleys each morning, the Yirgacheffe cherries ripen slowly — developing the floral jasmine and bright citrus complexity that defines our most celebrated roast. The soil remembers every rain, every drought, every season of patience.

We source exclusively from smallholder farms, where each lot is picked by hand and sun-dried on raised African beds. Traceability is not a marketing term here — it is a promise kept, harvest after harvest.

03
Brewing Ritual

Slow Pour.
Silent Morning.

The pour-over is not a technique — it is a meditation. The gooseneck arc of water over freshly ground bloom. The 30-second pause as the bed exhales its gases. The spiral pour that coaxes flavour evenly from every particle. Time slows here. Intentionally.

We recommend a 1:15 ratio — one gram of coffee to fifteen grams of water at 93°C — as a starting point for exploration. But ultimately, the perfect cup lives in your own ritual, refined brew by brew.

Method
V60 / Pour-Over
Ratio
1 : 15
Water Temp
92 – 94°C
Brew Time
3 – 3.5 min
Chapter 04 — Brand Philosophy

At the Intersection of
Craft & Emotion

Velvet Crema was born from a simple but profound conviction: that coffee, at its highest expression, is an art form. Not one that demands reverence from afar — but one that invites you inward, into its warmth, its complexity, its quiet ceremony.

Every decision we make — the farms we source from, the temperature curves we coax our roasters through, the vessels we choose to deliver your beans in — is guided by one question: Does this honour the cup?
